We believe in connecting people to more than just movies. See The Vision

Established in 2019, CineMunch® is a luxury restaurant and movie theater brand. Our mission is to provide an elevated experience from the traditional movie-going experience. For the main show, step into our boutique theatre showing the latest movies. Then get acquainted with our thoughtfully prepared and seasonally inspired menu, prepared with a drink from our mixologist. All of this is from our website.
